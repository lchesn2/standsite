Hello

I’m Howard, the founder of HNL

A brand born from a simple vision: to make working on the go as comfortable and efficient as any office. I started my journey out as a contract worker, constantly shifting between locations, I knew there had to be a better way to set up a professional workspace anywhere, and I couldn’t find a solution that fit. So, I created one. LiteLift is the result of countless hours of design, testing, and refining to bring something light, sturdy, and portable into the world. Every detail is crafted with purpose, aiming to support anyone who needs flexibility in their work life. My hope is that LiteLift helps you work better, wherever life takes you.
